BTS Achievements

K-pop boy band BTS won big at the 2018 Mnet Asian Music Awards (MAMA), Friday, picking up two of the top three music awards, leading a couple of the seven member group to burst into tears. At the end of the 2018 MAMA Hong Kong held in the AsiaWorld Arena, BTS had been awarded Album of the Year for "Love Yourself: Tear" released in May, as well as Artist of the Year.

BTS has bagged three awards, the 'Favorite Pop Group' award for the third consecutive year, 'Favorite Pop Song' with the group's second English-language single 'Butter,' and the group's first-ever 'Artist of the Year' award! BTS is the first and only Asian act to win the 'Artist of the Year' award at the American Music Awards. The members show gratitude in their speeches, especially for ARMYs.
